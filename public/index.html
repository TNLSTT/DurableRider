DurableRider

DurableRider turns raw Strava activity webhooks into reliable, durable data pipelines. We queue, reprocess, and harden every ride so you can ship athlete features without losing sleep over dropped events.

Connect with Strava See how it works

Durable ingest

Activities stream in from Strava and land in a resilient queue backed by Redis and BullMQ. No more missed rides when Strava hiccups.

Auto-retry processing

Each activity flows through our worker that enriches, sanitizes, and persists final metrics into Postgres or S3 with built-in retry logic.

Instant webhooks

OAuth, verification, and webhook endpoints are ready out of the box so you can plug DurableRider directly into your athlete experience.
