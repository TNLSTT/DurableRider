DurableRider

DurableRider listens to your Strava webhooks, rebuilds each activity with durability analytics, and writes the finished metrics straight back to your feed. Every ride ends up with fatigue-aware power breakdowns, efficiency trends, cadence stability windows, and a durability score you can trust.

Connect with Strava See the metrics

Durability Score overlay

We combine power fade, heart-rate drift, and efficiency decline into a single 0–100 durability score that posts to the activity description so athletes can compare rides at a glance.

Power fade breakdown

Quartile power tables highlight how output changes from the opening hour to the final stretch, showing when fatigue started to bite and how much power was lost.

Core durability telemetry

Power–heart rate decoupling, cadence stability windows, and dual-sided torque smoothness reveal when the kinetic chain starts leaking force upstream of the crank.

Fatigue-resistance curve

Rolling best-effort charts for 5, 10, 20, and 60 minutes land as images in your Strava gallery so riders can see how deep into the ride they can surge.
